Research output on retrieval-augmented generation, low-resource NLP, and legal AI. Most of it traces back to a problem I hit while shipping something.

2025

Enhancing Retrieval and Re-ranking in RAG: A Case Study on Tax Law of Azerbaijan

Z. Rustamov, M. Gasimzade, S. Rustamov

IEEE 19th International Conference on Application of Information and Communication Technologies (AICT 2025)

A case study on retrieval-augmented generation for Azerbaijani tax law. Compares hybrid retrieval (dense + sparse), Reciprocal Rank Fusion variants, and reranking strategies on a corpus of legislative articles and real legal queries.

2024

Contextualized Word Embeddings in Azerbaijani Language

T. Alizada, U. Suleymanov, Z. Rustamov

IEEE 18th International Conference on Application of Information and Communication Technologies (AICT 2024) · cited 4×

Pretraining and fine-tuning transformer models (RoBERTa, GPT-2) on curated Azerbaijani datasets to produce contextualized embeddings, contributing toward closing the resource gap for Azerbaijani-language NLP.

2024

Integrating Attention Mechanisms with LSTM for Azerbaijani Spelling Correction

Z. Rustamov, E. Mammadli, I. Hasanli, H. Ismayilzada, S. Rustamov

International Conference on Modeling, Simulation and Optimization (Springer), pp. 534–547

An LSTM-based Seq2Seq model with attention for Azerbaijani spelling correction. Trained on 92,500 sentence pairs from social media, the system reaches 97.4% accuracy on the test set and outperforms existing tools on morphologically complex inputs.

LSTM Seq2Seq Attention Azerbaijani

2024

Analysis of Public Sentiment in Azerbaijani News and Social Media

N. Guliyev, Z. Rustamov, S. Rustamov

Proceedings of the International Conference on Computing, Machine Learning (ACM) · cited 4×

Sentiment analysis across 38,000 social-media comments and 12,000 news articles in Azerbaijani. Benchmarks BERT, GPT-3.5-Turbo, LSTM, SVM, and Logistic Regression with multiple vectorizers. SVM consistently outperforms other models across both content types.
