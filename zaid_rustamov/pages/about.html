About

AI Engineer and Researcher

Zaid Rustamov is an AI Engineer at MegaSec AI Laboratory and a Researcher at the Center of Data Analytics and Research at ADA University.

Background

He works on the design and deployment of advanced AI systems across legal, civic, and data-driven domains. His expertise includes Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Generative AI, with a focus on building scalable, production-grade systems and end-to-end AI pipelines. His work combines research and engineering to develop robust solutions that perform effectively in real-world environments.

What I Do

  • Build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems
  • Design multi-agent AI pipelines
  • Develop hybrid search systems (dense + sparse)
  • Optimize AI for low-resource languages (Azerbaijani)
  • Create production-ready AI architectures

Core Expertise

  • Legal AI Systems
  • Multi-Agent Architectures
  • Information Retrieval & Ranking
  • Natural Language Processing
  • AI System Design & Optimization

Vision

I believe AI systems should not only perform well in controlled environments but also function reliably in real-world conditions.

My goal is to build robust, interpretable, and scalable AI systems that improve access to information and public services.
