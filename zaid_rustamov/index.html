I build production retrieval systems for legal, civic, and low-resource language domains — where the model is the easy part and the pipeline is everything.

AI Engineer at MegaSec AI Laboratory and Researcher at the Center of Data Analytics and Research at ADA University. My work centers on retrieval-augmented generation, multi-agent architectures, and Azerbaijani NLP.

The systems I ship aren’t just demos. They handle real legal queries, route citizen requests, and run inside ministries — which means the engineering question is always the same: how do you keep retrieval quality up as the domain shifts and the data drifts?
